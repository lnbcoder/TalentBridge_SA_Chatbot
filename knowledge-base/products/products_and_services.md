TALENTBRIDGE SA — PRODUCTS & SERVICES
=======================================

OVERVIEW
--------
TalentBridge SA offers three core product lines designed to create value for graduates,
partner companies, and the broader South African economy. Every product is built on
the principle that graduates must be skilled, certified, and project-experienced before
they meet a hiring company.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
PRODUCT 1: TALENTCONNECT — Graduate Placement Service
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT IT IS
----------
TalentConnect is TalentBridge SA's flagship employment placement product. It allows
companies to browse, filter, and select pre-vetted, trained, and certified university
graduates for internship, contract, or permanent employment opportunities.

HOW IT WORKS FOR COMPANIES
---------------------------
1. Company registers as a TalentBridge Partner (free registration)
2. Company accesses the TalentConnect portal and applies filters:
   - Discipline (e.g., Software Development, Finance, Engineering)
   - Skills (e.g., Python, Power BI, AutoCAD)
   - Certifications (e.g., AWS, CCNA, CFA Level 1)
   - Location (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Remote)
   - Availability (Immediate, 30 days, 60 days)
   - Qualification level (Diploma, Degree, Honours)
   - Experience (No experience, 0–1 year, 1–2 years, 2+ years)
3. Company shortlists preferred candidates and requests interviews
4. TalentBridge coordinates the interview process
5. Company makes an offer; TalentBridge facilitates onboarding handover

HOW IT BENEFITS GRADUATES
--------------------------
- Graduate profiles are visible to 80+ partner companies nationally
- TalentBridge negotiates fair, market-related salary packages
- Graduates receive interview preparation and coaching
- 90-day post-placement support: TalentBridge checks in with both parties
- Replacement guarantee protects the graduate too (structured exit if the fit is wrong)

PRICING (COMPANIES)
-------------------
- Basic Access (Free): Browse profiles, submit 2 interview requests/month
- Pro Plan (R3,500/month): Unlimited browsing, 10 interview requests/month, priority matching
- Enterprise Plan (R8,000/month): Dedicated account manager, bulk hiring, priority shortlisting
- Placement Fee (Permanent hire): 8% of first-year gross salary (industry standard 15–20%)

PRODUCT 2: BUILDLAB — Project Team Assembly Service
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT IT IS
----------
BuildLab enables companies, startups, NGOs, and government departments to submit
project briefs and have TalentBridge assemble a team of skilled graduates to execute
the project. This gives graduates real-world project experience while delivering
tangible value to clients.

HOW IT WORKS
------------
1. Client submits a Project Brief via the TalentBridge portal or by contacting
   the Business Development team
2. TalentBridge Project Manager reviews the brief and scopes the work
3. A graduate team is assembled based on the required skills
4. Client approves team composition and project plan
5. Project is executed in Agile sprints with weekly status reports to the client
6. Final deliverable is handed over; client reviews and signs off
7. All team members earn a verified project badge on their TalentBridge profiles

TYPES OF PROJECTS ACCEPTED
----------------------------
- Software/App Development (web apps, mobile apps, APIs)
- Data Analysis & Dashboard Development (Power BI, Tableau, Python)
- Digital Marketing Campaigns (social media, SEO, content marketing)
- Brand Identity & Graphic Design projects
- Business Process Analysis & Documentation
- Financial Modelling & Reporting
- Network Infrastructure Planning & Documentation
- Research Reports & Market Studies
- E-Commerce Store Setup & Optimisation
- AI/ML Proof-of-Concept models

GRADUATE BENEFITS FROM BUILDLAB
---------------------------------
- Real client projects added to their professional portfolio
- Earn TalentBridge Project Completion Certificate per project
- Paid a project stipend (R3,000 – R8,000 per project depending on scope)
- Exposure to client-facing professional environments
- Projects often lead directly to employment offers from the client

PRICING (CLIENTS)
-----------------
- Small Projects (under 4 weeks, 1–2 graduates): from R12,000
- Medium Projects (4–8 weeks, 3–5 graduates): from R35,000
- Large Projects (8–16 weeks, 5–10 graduates): from R75,000
- Enterprise Projects: custom quote

PRODUCT 3: SKILLBRIDGE — Graduate Upskilling Programme
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT IT IS
----------
SkillBridge is TalentBridge SA's internal skills development programme that all
graduates must complete before being listed on TalentConnect. It is also available
as a standalone programme for companies wanting to upskill their existing graduate
employees.

PROGRAMME STRUCTURE (For TalentBridge Graduates)
--------------------------------------------------
Phase 1 — Professional Readiness (2 weeks)
  - Workplace communication and business etiquette
  - CV, LinkedIn, and personal branding masterclass
  - Interview skills and salary negotiation
  - Financial literacy (budgeting, tax, RA contributions)

Phase 2 — Technical Upskilling (2 weeks)
  - Discipline-specific technical training (see tracks below)
  - Tools and software relevant to the graduate's chosen field
  - Hands-on exercises and mini-projects

Phase 3 — Certification Preparation (1 week)
  - Guided study for an industry certification exam
  - Mock exams and revision sessions
  - TalentBridge covers the cost of the first certification attempt

Phase 4 — Project Simulation (1 week)
  - Team-based simulated client project
  - Deliverable presented to a panel of TalentBridge mentors
  - Feedback, iteration, and final submission

AVAILABLE UPSKILLING TRACKS
-----------------------------
- Software Development (Python, JavaScript, React, Node.js)
- Data Science & Analytics (Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau)
- Cloud & DevOps (AWS, Azure, Docker, Terraform)
- Cybersecurity (CompTIA Security+, ethical hacking tools)
- Digital Marketing (Google Ads, SEO, Meta Ads, HubSpot)
- Financial Analysis (Excel modelling, Power BI, IFRS basics)
- UX/UI Design (Figma, user research methods)
- Project Management (Agile/Scrum, PMP preparation)
- Business Analysis (BPMN, requirements docs, ECBA prep)
- Mobile Development (Flutter, React Native)

CORPORATE SKILLBRIDGE (For Company Employees)
----------------------------------------------
Companies can enrol their existing graduate employees in SkillBridge tracks to
rapidly upskill their workforce. Corporate cohorts of 10–30 employees can be
accommodated. Certification costs included for corporate packages.

Pricing: From R18,000 per participant (full 6-week programme)
          From R7,500 per participant (single track, 2 weeks)
