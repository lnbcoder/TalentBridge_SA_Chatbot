COMPANY OVERVIEW — TALENTBRIDGE SA
===================================

BUSINESS MODEL
--------------
TalentBridge SA operates a three-sided marketplace:

1. GRADUATES — University graduates register on the platform, undergo skills assessments, complete training programs, earn certificates, and build portfolio projects. They become available for placement.

2. COMPANIES — Partner companies browse vetted graduate profiles, filter by skills, qualifications, and availability, and request graduates for internships, projects, or full-time roles.

3. PROJECTS — Companies submit project briefs. TalentBridge SA assembles graduate teams to execute the projects, giving graduates real experience while delivering value to clients.

REVENUE STREAMS
---------------
- Placement Fees: Companies pay a placement fee when they hire a TalentBridge graduate full-time.
- Project Contracts: Revenue from client project execution, shared between TalentBridge and the graduate team.
- Training Partnerships: Partnerships with certification bodies (Microsoft, AWS, Google, CompTIA) where TalentBridge facilitates enrolment.
- Subscription Plans: Companies pay monthly/annual subscriptions for premium access to the graduate talent pool.
