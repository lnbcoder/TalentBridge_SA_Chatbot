CAREERS AT TALENTBRIDGE SA
===========================

JOIN THE TALENTBRIDGE FAMILY
-----------------------------
Whether you are a fresh graduate looking to launch your career, or a company searching for top-tier talent, TalentBridge SA has a pathway for you.

FOR GRADUATES — HOW TO JOIN
-----------------------------
Step 1: APPLICATION
  - Visit www.talentbridgesa.co.za/apply
  - Submit your CV, academic transcript, and a 2-minute video introduction
  - Minimum requirement: A completed 3-year university degree or National Diploma (NQF Level 6+)

Step 2: SKILLS ASSESSMENT
  - Online aptitude and skills assessment (60 minutes)
  - Discipline-specific test (e.g., coding challenge for developers, case study for business analysts)
  - Soft skills and communication evaluation

Step 3: ONBOARDING PROGRAMME (6 Weeks)
  - Week 1–2: Professional development (communication, workplace readiness, business etiquette)
  - Week 3–4: Technical upskilling in your chosen discipline
  - Week 5–6: Real project simulation and portfolio building

Step 4: CERTIFICATION
  - Complete at least one industry certification relevant to your field
  - TalentBridge covers the cost of the first certification exam

Step 5: PLACEMENT
  - Profile activated on the TalentBridge platform
  - Matched with partner companies based on skills and preferences
  - Placement support throughout the interview and offer process
