MISSION STATEMENT
-----------------
TalentBridge SA exists to close the gap between educated graduates and meaningful employment. We empower young South African university graduates by equipping them with real-world skills, certifications, and project experience — then connecting them directly with companies looking for capable, vetted talent.

VISION
------
To be Africa's most trusted graduate talent pipeline, producing skilled, certified, and project-ready professionals who drive innovation across industries.

WHAT WE DO
----------
TalentBridge SA is a graduate talent development and placement company. We recruit top university graduates across South Africa, provide structured upskilling programs, and connect them with partner companies for internships, contract work, and full-time employment.

We are not a staffing agency. We are a talent incubator. Every graduate on our platform has undergone skills training, real-world project exposure, and certification — making them job-ready from day one.

CORE VALUES

4. Innovation – We embrace technology and modern skills development.
5. Ubuntu – We grow together as a community.

HISTORY
-------
TalentBridge SA was founded in 2018 by Lerato Mokoena and Sipho Dlamini, two former HR professionals who recognized a critical disconnect: companies struggled to find skilled graduates, while thousands of qualified graduates struggled to find jobs. TalentBridge SA was built to solve both problems simultaneously.

Starting with just 12 graduates in a Johannesburg co-working space, the company has grown to a national network of over 300 active graduates across multiple disciplines including Software Development, Data Science, Finance, Marketing, Engineering, and Business Analysis.
